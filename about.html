About us

Evansys Technologies is a Training, development and consulting division.It was started in the year 2008 by few like-minded people, who were able to see the demand of IT solutions and to fulfill the growing manpower requirement of the organization as well as to facilitate the growing demand of skillful design engineers in the Electronic Hardware design area. We are familiar with corporate sector because of the quality services, which we have been providing to our clients over these years.
Evansys Technologies have networked with companies to help job seekers in their relevant or desired field. Special training would be given to candidates to equip themselves to fit a perfect job profile or a company.
We are also providing separate training in software languages and developing technologies are being successfully conducted by us and which ends not only in issuing an authorized certificate, but also in a combo of offer letter with the best package available in the industry.
